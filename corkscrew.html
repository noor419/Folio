Team Design Project

 

This project focused on an FEA analysis on a deformed object. For our purposes, a ductile corkscrew was put under loading to determine actual needed load for the deformation. This value was used to conduct an FEA study on the deformed handle, whose design was then optimized by the addition of fillets to reduce failure.
Date:  March 2019 - May 2019

Skillsets:  Finite Element Analysis (FEA)


Software Used:
      • Solidworks
      • Solidworks Simulation
